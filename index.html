About Us

A dedicated detail shop, built for the South Bay

Splash Auto Detail is a fixed-location detail shop in Chula Vista's Eastlake district, serving the South Bay since 2011. We provide expert car wash and detailing for every kind of vehicle, from daily commuters to luxury automobiles, with interior and exterior detail packages at our dedicated facility.

Fifteen years in, our shop has built a loyal local customer base serving sedans, trucks, SUVs, luxury cars, and EVs alike, all backed by a 5.0-star reputation across 66 Yelp reviews.

FAQ

Good to know before you visit

A few common questions from South Bay drivers booking a wash or detail.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Give us a call at (619) 993-8536 before you head over, we can confirm current availability and let you know the best time to bring your vehicle in during our posted hours.

Do you detail EVs and luxury vehicles?

Yes. We regularly detail sedans, trucks, SUVs, EVs, and luxury vehicles, our services are built to handle every kind of vehicle that comes through the lot.

What's the difference between a wash and a full detail?

A car wash covers a thorough exterior clean starting at $29. A full detail goes deeper, interior and exterior packages, wash and wax, or nano ceramic coating for longer-lasting protection. Call us and we'll help you pick the right package for your vehicle.

What are your hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 8:15am to 5:00pm, and Saturday from 8:15am to 2:00pm. We're closed on Sunday.

Where are you located?

Our dedicated facility is at 851 District Pl in the Eastlake neighborhood of Chula Vista, convenient to the greater San Diego South Bay.
